Marine Protected Area - Cook Islands Marine Park

Marine Protected Areas (MPAs) are portions of the ocean that are to be kept in protection for long-term conservation goals. According to the International Union for Conservation of Nature (IUCN, 2022), "[MPAs] are the only mainstream conservation-focused, area-based measure to increase the quality and extent of ocean protection." MPAs are extremely important as they significantly aid in climate change resilience, as well as rebuilding ecological environments. It is sad to note that the MPAs currently established do not have enough human and financial resources to take the full measures to properly care, protect, and manage these invaluable areas.
